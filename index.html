Why choose us

Circuitboard Education is a newly formed consulting partnership devoted to bringing new media tools and games into a classroom environment. We are an adaptable team that wants to help teachers and schools design and implement curricula surrounding games and digital technologies. We look forward to using our knowledge and experience to improve your classroom experience for your students!


Circuitboard Classroom Values

  • Engagement. We expect active participation from our students.
  • Openness, diversity, and acceptance. We value a diversity of viewpoints in the classroom.
  • Difficulty. We present challenging material and believe we learn more from our failures than our successes.
  • Community. We encourage students to help each other and to learn from each other.
  • Critical-Thought. We believe whole-heartedly in the value of critical thinking over rote skills. Multi-level analysis is encouraged and rewarded.
